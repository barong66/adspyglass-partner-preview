Pre-integration: who it's for

A fit check before you spend time on direct integration.

You're a fit if
  • · You're an ad network or DSP buying traffic and looking for premium publisher inventory
  • · You have (or can build) a REST API exposing zones, ad code, and statistics
  • · You're prepared to commit a $1,000 deposit + 5% rev share
You're not a fit if
  • · You don't have an API for zones, ad code, or statistics
  • · You're not ready to commit the $1,000 deposit
  • · Your offer coverage is too narrow to fill meaningful traffic volume

How it works

From application to live in the catalog. Roughly two weeks if your API matches our spec.

  1. 1

    Submit your application

    Use the form below. Tell us about your network, traffic volume, and inventory types.

  2. 2

    Terms review — 2 business days

    Our BD team confirms partnership terms and requests technical contacts on your side.

  3. 3

    Pay the $1,000 deposit

    We send the invoice. Technical integration begins as soon as payment is received.

  4. 4

    Technical integration — 5 business days

    Our engineers plug into your API: zones, ad code, statistics.

  5. 5

    Go live in the catalog

    Your inventory becomes available to AdSpyglass publishers. First check-in scheduled at week 2 to review performance.

What we need from your API

1. Sites & zones list

The publisher sites you operate, each with their ad zones — zone identifier and a human-readable name per zone.

2. Ad code for each zone

HTML snippet or URL for serving the zone. Must support {SUB_ID} and {KEYWORDS} macros so we can substitute tracking and targeting values before rendering.

3. Traffic statistics

Impressions, clicks, and revenue per zone — grouped by date, country, and sub_id. Field names in your response can be anything (shows, views, earnings, etc.) as long as these dimensions are covered.
