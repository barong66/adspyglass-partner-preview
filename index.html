What we need from your API

Three pieces of data over HTTPS. Path names, parameter names, and response shape are up to you — what matters is that this information is available.

Step 1

Sites & zones list

The publisher sites you operate, each with their ad zones — zone identifier and a human-readable name per zone.

We fetch this once on integration and refresh occasionally as your inventory changes.

Step 2

Ad code for each zone

HTML snippet or URL for serving the zone. Must support {SUB_ID} and {KEYWORDS} macros — we substitute tracking and targeting values before rendering.

Macros are critical: without them we lose source attribution and contextual targeting.

Step 3

Traffic statistics

Impressions, clicks, and revenue per zone — grouped by date, country, and sub_id. Field names can be anything (shows, views, earnings).

Pulled nightly to reconcile billing and show publishers their performance.
